    chapathi:

  • chapathi is a type of unleavened flatbread originating from the indian subcontinent, made from whole wheat flour and water, rolled into thin discs and cooked on a hot griddle or tawa.

  • it is a staple food in many parts of india and is often served with various curries, vegetables, or lentil dishes.

  • chapathi is known for its soft texture and is typically enjoyed with a variety of accompaniments, making it a versatile and popular choice in indian cuisine.

    paneer tikka:

  • paneer tikka is a popular indian appetizer made from marinated cubes of paneer (Indian cottage cheese) that are skewered and grilled or baked until charred and flavorful.

  • the marinade typically includes yogurt, spices, and herbs, giving the paneer a rich and smoky flavor.

  • it is often served with mint chutney and is a favorite among vegetarians, making it a staple in indian restaurants and homes.
